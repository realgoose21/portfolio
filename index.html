Jinhong Park

Hello! I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Urban Administration at the University of Seoul. My research focuses on Urban Data Science & AI, Pedestrian Policy Assessment, Quantitative Research, and GIS & Network Analysis.

I am particularly interested in how urban environments interact with human behaviors (like pedestrian movement and traffic accidents) and how we can use data-driven approaches to solve complex urban challenges.
